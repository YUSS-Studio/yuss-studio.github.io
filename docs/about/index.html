About YUSS

YUSS is a collective of designers who met during their Graphic Design MFA program at ArtCenter College of Design in Pasadena, California. We have worked together for almost three years since moving to Los Angeles and have gained a deep understanding of the city. We believe this perspective makes our team uniquely qualified to design the identity for the 2025 AIGA National Conference. Thank you for considering our portfolio submission.

Capabilities

brand identity, packaging, publication, wayfinding, type design, art direction, 2D/3D motion graphics, copywriting, interactive installations, digital design, creative coding, software development
