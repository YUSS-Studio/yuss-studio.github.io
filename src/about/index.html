About YUSS

YUSS is a collective of designers who met during their Graphic Design MFA program at ArtCenter College of Design in Pasadena, California. We have worked together for almost three years since moving to Los Angeles and have gained a deep understanding of the city. Shakeel on our team has been involved with AIGA for four years in numerous local and national events.

Capabilities

Brand identity, packaging, publication design, wayfinding, art direction, type design, 2D/3D motion graphics, copywriting, interactive installations, digital design, creative coding, software development


We’d love to discuss your project, please reach out at yuss@applieddesigner.com.
